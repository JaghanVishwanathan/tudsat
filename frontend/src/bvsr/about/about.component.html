Budesverband Studentischer Raumfahrt (BVSR)

What is BVSR?

BVSR (Bundesverband studentischer Raumfahrt e.V.) is the German federation of student-led aerospace associations.
Founded in 2021, BVSR unites more than 15 university groups across Germany to:
  • Promote aerospace education and innovation.
  • Encourage collaboration across student projects.
  • Represent student interests in research, industry, and policy discussions
